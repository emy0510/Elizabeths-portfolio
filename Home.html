About Me

My name is Elizabeth Yantis. I have been a professional photographer for 7 years but I have loved photography since I was young. Anywhere I would go I would have a camera with me taking pictures of everything I could see- from pretty flowers to the horse behind my grandmothers house to dragging my friends and family into impromtu photoshoots.

I am based out of Arlington, Texas and I love to travel to capture the beautiful and important moments in the world around me. I love capturing the important moments in people's lives. I shoot everything from engagement photos to graduation pictures to all the crazy creative ideas my clients come up with!

I am known for my:

  • Client Relationships- When you feel comfortable you look comfortable
  • Editing Styles- I tend to have lots of colors in my pictures
  • Posing ideas- I want you to look your best in all your pictures
  • Location scouting- A beautiful location can really elevate a picture
  • Editing Time- I want you to see your pictures as soon as possible
  • Attention to Detail- I make sure that everything is perfect before we start shooting
  • Creative ideas- I have so many creative ideas that I would love to execute
  • Willingness to try almost anything- I will to try almost any idea that a client wants to try
Please browse my portfolio and other galleries and reach out with questions or for booking information.
