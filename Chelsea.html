Chelsea

Who you did the work for/with:

I worked with Chelsea Ghormley, a person I use to work with.

Approximately a paragraph explanation of the goals of the project:

This shoot was all about playing with fun textures and colors. She had just dyed her hair and wanted the setting to match.
We found the SweetTooth Hotel in Dallas that had great reviews for being a fun colorful art installation with lots of photo ops and
we knew it was the perfect place for us.

Your role in the project

I was the creative directer, photographer, and editor.

Collaborators:

I did not collaborate with another photographer but I always try to collaborate with the modles to an extent.

Start and end times:

8/10/23- 8/15/23

Personal insights gained:

I have never done a shoot that was focused on colors and had so many involved.
Each room created a different challenge, lighting, colors, textures, vibes were all different room to room
which created some really unique pictures.

Challenges encountered:

Since each room had drastically different lighting, camera settings and editing were a little challenging.

Things that you tried but did not work:

There were poses that one of us thought would look good that turned out to look a little bit awkward.

What you might do differently:

I am really satisfied with how these pictures turned out and I would not change anything.

What you accomplished:

I successfully worked with techniques that were out of my comfort zone in a place that I wasn't familliar with that had a wide variety of lighting.

Main thing you learned from the project:

I learned that playing with lighting and editing techniques can be really fun!
