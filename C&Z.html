Catherine and Zack

Who you did the work for/with:

I worked with the couple, Zack and Catherine

Approximately a paragraph explanation of the goals of the shoot:

The goal of this shoot from the client was to get cute, Instagramable pictures.
I did that by asking them to cordinate their outfits and took them to a cute lake.
For about half the time I gave them poses to do then the other half I gave them prompts like "chase her", "twirll her" "sneak up behind him and hug him from behind"
so that I could get real, candid moments.

Your role in the project

I was the creative directer, photographer and editor.

Collaborators:

I did not collaborate with anyone on this shoot

Start and end times:

Start: 10/08/22
End: 10/15/22

Challenges encountered:

One of the most challenging parts of this experience was making them comfortable in front of the camera.
They were both pretty stiff when we started so the earlier shots look very uncomfortable.

Things that you tried but did not work:

I tried them in different poses that I have seen online and some of them worked for what they were going for and some of them didn't.
They were very vocal about the poses that they felt uncomfortable in.

What you might do differently:

If and when I work with them again, I would give them more candid opportunities earlier in the session. Some of their favorite pictures were ones that were candids of them interacting with eachother

What you accomplished:

I successfully made a couple who does not take pictures often relax and become comfortable and confident in front of a camera.

Main thing you learned from the project:

I learned that part of being a photographer who photographs people is all about working on the fly. Being adaptable is very important.
